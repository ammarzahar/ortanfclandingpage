Why Local Academies Matter

In countries like the Netherlands, football development thrives because academies are everywhere — often just minutes from a child’s home. Football becomes routine, not a luxury.

Ortan was built on this belief. Our mission is simple: bring structured, high-quality football academies closer to where children live.

By empowering local partners, Ortan creates neighbourhood-based academies that parents trust, kids attend consistently, and communities support.

"Great football nations are built at the neighbourhood level."

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be a football coach to become a partner?
No. You do not need to be a coach. You can operate as an owner while Ortan provides coaching frameworks and training support.
Can I run the academy part-time?
Yes. Many partners start part-time and scale gradually, depending on their commitment and location demand.
Does Ortan provide marketing support?
Yes. Ortan provides brand assets, marketing guidance, and campaign templates to help you attract players locally.
How many players do I need to start?
You can start with a small group. Many partners begin with 2–5 players and grow over time.
What kind of support will I receive from HQ?
Partners receive curriculum access, coach training, operational SOPs, and ongoing guidance from the Ortan HQ team.
Can I open more than one location in the future?
Yes. High-performing partners may expand to additional locations subject to approval and capacity.
Is this a franchise or a partnership?
This is a partnership-based model focused on long-term growth, not a one-time franchise sale.
