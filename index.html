Frequently Asked Questions

What should my child wear for the trial?

Comfortable sports attire (jersey/shorts), football boots (preferred) or sports shoes, and a water bottle.

Do you have classes if it rains?

Light rain continues. In case of heavy rain or lightning, we pause the session. If a session is cancelled completely, we provide a replacement class.

Can girls join?

Absolutely! We are an inclusive academy with many girls training across our different age categories.
